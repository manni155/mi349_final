About Thomas

Thomas Manning is a Senior at Michigan State University studying Information Science. Raised in southeast Michigan, he has been working with computers since adolescence. As a student, Thomas' main focuses are on UX design and research on artificial inteligence and machine learning.

Thomas has been producing a variety of art since he was younger. Originally releasing low quality videos to YouTube, he shifted his focus to music after becoming a musician in high school. Throughout college he began designing t-shirts, releasing more videos, and creating websites. He has plans to begin work on smaller video game projects.

Thomas has been involved in a variety of activities throughout his career. His main interest is competitive gaming, primarily the Super Smash Bros. series, in which he hosts events, tracks statistics, and competes. He is also an avid musician, playing several instruments including the saxophone and guitar. He hopes to one day compete on Jeopardy, having tried out once before.
